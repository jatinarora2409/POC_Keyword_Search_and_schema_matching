1367 Alta Vista is a collection of twelve fitly designed modern condominiums just a few blocks from Hollywood's most famous landmarks and is only steps away from some of the best restaurants, cafes, bars and shops. Each thoughtfully designed residence is situated on a corner and draws an abundance of natural light from multiple directions, giving an airy, open plan interior with an inviting feel that connects to spacious balconies. Finishes are carefully selected to create a seamless flow between kitchen, dining and living areas with white oak hardwood floors, custom Italian cabinetry, stainless steel German appliances, Caesarstone counters, custom interior doors and modern fireplaces. Porcelain tile bathrooms with floating vanities, bathtubs and custom built closets complete the Master suites. The unit has outstanding City and Hollywood Hills views, for lounging, dining and entertaining.
