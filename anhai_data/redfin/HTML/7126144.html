Sited at the end of a private cul-de-sac in the heart of celebrity row, this 4-bed, 4.5-bath, gated, modern home was designed by Ralph Yanagawa, AIA, has recently undergone a meticulous restoration and offers California living at its finest. From the entry hall, ascend a flight of stairs to a dramatic living room, fireplace, den, and bar with an abundance of natural light, floor to ceiling Fleetwood doors and windows. The chef’s kitchen has a Miele coffee system, Gaggenau wine storage, Dacor appliances and walk-in pantry. The generous master suite includes a sitting area, walk-in closet, and travertine stone master bath with Waterworks tub, overlooking the city below. Downstairs is a Dolby Atmos screening room and two additional ensuite bedrooms, one used as a private gym. Additional features include: Savant home automation, Lutron lighting and shades, oversized 2-car garage, plus ample off-street parking. Professionally landscaped gardens are highlighted by a pool and spa.
