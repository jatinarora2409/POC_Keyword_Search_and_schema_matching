The perfect and most convenient location in Friendship Heights. This studio condominium has a gourmet kitchen with Viking appliances, granite countertops, custom made Murphy bed (matches the kitchen cabinets), walk-in closet, and Bosch washer and dryer in the unit. The 1st class building features a renovated fitness room, party/meeting room, and a beautiful lobby with concierge. Close to Friendship Heights Metro and all the wonderful shopping and restaurants nearby.
