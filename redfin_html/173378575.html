Behind this gorgeously-preserved Edwardian building in Russian Hill lies newly-remodeled and modernized interiors. 1336A Green, a newly developed residence, was designed to optimize modern living with three full-sized bedrooms on one level, including a Primary Suite and guest bath. Featuring an open floor plan with high ceilings, the effect is awe-inspiring and dramatic. A single family alternative, this residence has it's own private entrance and over-sized sliding glass doors that walk out to a deeded patio, deck, & shared yard. Wonderful Russian Hill location with close proximity to both Polk & Hyde Street shops and restaurants. Shared roof deck with panoramic views of the Golden Gate Bridge, Marin Headlands & North Bay.
