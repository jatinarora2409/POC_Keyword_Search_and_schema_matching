This magnificent and elegant Transitional style home is situated in one of LAs most sought-after neighborhoods, moments from Melrose Ave. Blending traditional charm with modern European finishes, the home showcases richly finished design and contemporary amenities. Pass through the gated entry to find light, understated interiors, custom wainscotting, and wide-plank light wood floors that bring a handcrafted warmth. The gourmet kitchen boasts Wolf appliances, a breakfast nook and custom cabinetry. 5 bedrooms include a spacious master with an antique mirrored fireplace, two showroom closets, marble steam shower and soaking tub. Sliding pocket doors open to the backyard with privacy-enhancing hedges, pool & spa. The SmartHome-equipped residence also features a wood-paneled office, theater, fire pit and 2-car garage with car lift. Situated amidst West Hollywoods most popular restaurants and shops, minutes from Beverly Hills and the Sunset Strip.
