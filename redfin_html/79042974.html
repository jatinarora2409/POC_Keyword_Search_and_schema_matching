Situated in the heart of West Hollywood, this modern architectural townhome is one of five residences at Stanview Court. Built in 2014, and located on a coveted tree-lined street between Santa Monica Blvd and Melrose Avenue, the home boasts three stories of light-filled, open-plan living spaces, with wide-plank hardwood floors and floor-to-ceiling windows. The great room boasts spacious living and dining spaces, and a European-style kitchen with Caesarstone countertops and premier stainless-steel appliances. Upstairs, find three ensuite bedrooms, the elegant owner's retreat, featuring a soaking tub and shower, dual vanities, private balcony, and sweeping Hollywood Hills views. The rooftop terrace with multi-directional views is ideal for lounging and entertaining. Additional features include a main floor powder room, a dual-zone HVAC system, and 2 gated parking spaces. Located moments from Whole Foods, Beverly Grove, and world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment.
